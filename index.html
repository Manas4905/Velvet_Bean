Our Story

Born From a Single Obsession: the Perfect Cup

In 2015, founder Arjun Mehta left a corporate career armed with a hand-grinder, a moka pot, and an insatiable curiosity. After months in the Ethiopian highlands and Colombian mountains, Velvet Bean was born in a 200 sq ft Bengaluru garage.

Today we operate 8 locations across India — but every roast is still done in-house, every batch tasted by our team, and every cup served with the same obsessive care.

Direct trade with 14 farming families
SCA-certified roasters & baristas
Roasted-to-order subscription available
100% compostable in-store packaging
